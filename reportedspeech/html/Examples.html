Examples :



Direct : She said to me ,"I will meet you tomorrow."
Indirect : She told me that she would meet me the next day.

Direct : The teacher said to the boy ," Why do you come to school late always? "
Indirect : The teacher asked the boy why he came to school late always.

Direct : He said to him," Do you play cricket ? "
Indirect : He asked him whether he played cricket.

Direct : The judge said to police ," Call the next witness."
Indirect The judge ordered police to call the next witness.

Direct : The tourist said," How beautiful the Taj Mahal is ! "
Indirect : The tourist exclaimed that the Taj Mahal was very beautiful.
