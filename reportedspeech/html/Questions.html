Questions :

Two type Questions there.... 'wh' Questions and 'yes or no' Qusetions
Both Questions identifiy by'?'


Wh Questions :


identifiy key --- The Sentence Starts with WH Question words.

like ...what,when,where,which,who,whom,whose,why,how,how much,how many,etc..

Ex,
Direct : The teacher said to the boy ," Why do you come to school late always? "

Yes Or No Question :


identifiy key --- The Sentence Starts with Be verbs or Modal auxiliary

like ...is,am.was,were,are,will,would,can,could,may,might,shall,should...

Ex,
Direct : He said to him," Are you living here ? "
